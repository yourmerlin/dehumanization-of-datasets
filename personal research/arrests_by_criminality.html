ICE Apprehensions by Criminality Type — Oct 2022 – Mar 2026

Each line tracks monthly ICE arrests by the agency's own criminality classification.
“Other Immigration Violator” — people detained solely for violating immigration rules, with no criminal charge or conviction — collapsed under Biden, then surged 17× in the year after Trump’s inauguration.

Source: ICE Arrest Database (public records litigation) via the Deportation Data Project — 713,464 records, Oct 2022–Mar 2026.
Criminality categories are ICE’s own field labels: 1 Convicted Criminal, 2 Pending Criminal Charges, 3 Other Immigration Violator.
The database purposefully omits names, faces, and personal identifiers. The absence of data is not neutral.
Labels placed using a Voronoi-based directly-labelling algorithm (after H. Stevens, Observable, 2019).
